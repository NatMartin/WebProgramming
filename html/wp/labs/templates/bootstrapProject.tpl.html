Bootstrap Project

The goal of this project is to make your index work on mobile phones.

  1. Change the page headers to a jumbotron.
  2. Add a navbar to the top of your pages. The navbar should have your name as the navbar-brand, and your top level index as your home.
  3. Put the menu on the left hand side of the page and some text describing the index on the left. Divide the page 5 for the menu and 7 for the text.
  4. Remember to check your work into Git.
